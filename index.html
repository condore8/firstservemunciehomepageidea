"A place where the love of the game meets the warmth of community." — First Serve Muncie

First Serve Muncie is Muncie's home for tennis and pickleball — built for everyone from first-timers picking up a racquet to competitive players chasing their best game.

We believe sport is better when it's social. That's why everything we do — our courts, our programs, our people — is designed to make you feel at home from the moment you walk through the door.
